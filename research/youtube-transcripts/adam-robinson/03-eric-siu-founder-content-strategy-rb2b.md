- Traditional SEO is dying: impressions going up, clicks going down — the "open jaw" graph — it's a cigar butt business (a few more puffs left)
- The new play is **Search Everywhere Optimization**: YouTube, Reddit, Amazon, Pinterest, Instagram, X — not just Google

- Think of yourself as an "employee" of each platform: consistency + reliability = compounding organic reach
- Marketing School ranked #1 on ChatGPT for "best marketing podcast" — because of consistency, volume, and citations (2900 episodes), not LLM gaming tactics
- **Podcast as pillar content strategy**: record once → publish to YouTube → chop into short form → republish everywhere via GenSpark
