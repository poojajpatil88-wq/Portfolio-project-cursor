## Key Takeaways

- Three content types: Billion Dollar Challenge (docu-series), Talking Heads (LinkedIn video), Live Events/Webinars
- Three content pillars: **Connection, Education, Inspiration** — every video must do at least one
- 80/20 innovation rule: Keep 80% of what works, iterate 20% — never rest on laurels
- Story structure is three beats only: Hook → Educate → CTA (beginning, middle, end)
- iPhone + small tripod = 99% of what you need to start; story and audio beat gear every time
- AI tools (Google Gemini, Claude) can replace the role of a producer for ideation and story structuring
- Attribution for high-production video content is near-impossible — leading indicators (community trust, event recognition) are the proxy for ROI
- Start by documenting what you're already doing; "Grow in Public" is the content strategy
