## Key Takeaways

- 5 old rules died; 5 new rules replaced them
- SEO = "search everywhere optimization" (YouTube, Amazon, Pinterest, App Store, ChatGPT, Perplexity)
- B2B CTV (connected TV ads) is underpriced and high ROI right now
- Human + AI operator model: one person can become a 100x employee
- Creator-led B2B (like Adam Robinson) drives pipeline + offsets content costs via influencer deals
- Rule of 7 → Rule of 21 (or even 28) for marketing touch points
- 6–12 month window before everyone catches up — early movers capture 10x value
