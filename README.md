## Key Insights Across Transcripts

### 1. Three Video Types for B2B SaaS
- **Search-based videos** — rank for specific YouTube search terms ICP types; evergreen for 2–5+ years
- **Algorithm-based videos** — pushed by YouTube to homefeed; story-driven, broader reach
- **Bottom-of-funnel videos** — client interviews, case study breakdowns, product demos; fewer views but highest conversion

### 2. YouTube-First Approach
Don't repurpose podcasts or webinars. Create dedicated, YouTube-optimized educational content. Separate the podcast to its own channel.

### 3. Compounding Effect
Unlike LinkedIn posts (48hr shelf life) or ads (stop when budget stops), YouTube videos compound over years. Stack 10–20 evergreen videos and each one adds to total monthly reach.

### 4. Distribution System (2–3x faster results)
- Promote each video via LinkedIn post with hook excerpt
- Embed videos in relevant blog posts (improves Google ranking too)
- Build pre-call email sequences featuring your best 2–3 videos
- Embed case study videos on homepage, pricing page, case study page

### 5. Tracking Pipeline Influence
- UTM parameters on every YouTube link
- Self-reported attribution question on booking/signup forms: *"Which platforms have you consumed our content on?"*
- These two together reveal YouTube's true influence on pipeline

### 6. Realistic Timeline
- Month 1: Better organic performance than previous content
- Month 2–3: First leads from YouTube specifically
- Month 4–6: Consistent lead generation channel
- Month 6–12: Primary lead gen channel
